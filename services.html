Services

  • Group Rides

    Gather your friends and family, and set out on a group riding adventure like no other. Our guided group rides allow you to explore picturesque trails, bask in the beauty of nature, and create cherished memories together. Rest assured that our experienced guides will accompany you every step of the way, ensuring a safe and enjoyable ride for everyone in the group.

    Price: 1000 kr - 3000 kr per month

  • One-to-One Guided Horse Rides

    For a more personalized and tailored riding experience, our one-to-one guided rides are the perfect choice. Our team of expert instructors is dedicated to providing individualized attention, making it a fantastic learning opportunity for riders of all levels. Whether you're a novice looking to build your skills or an experienced rider seeking refinement, our one-to-one sessions offer the ideal platform to reach your equestrian goals.

    Price: 2500 kr per month

  • Small Horses for Small Riders

    We understand that young riders and petite equestrians may require a special touch when it comes to horse riding. That's why we've carefully curated a selection of small and gentle horses that are perfectly suited for kids or those of smaller stature. Our commitment to safety and fun remains paramount, ensuring that even our youngest riders have the opportunity to connect with these magnificent animals and experience the thrill of horseback riding.

    Price: 2000 kr per month
